Welcome to my minimalist website!

I am a Postdoctoral Fellow in the Environmental Statistics Research Group at KAUST, in Saudi Arabia 🇸🇦. I am fortunate enough to have a job that really fulfills me, and to live on the beautiful KAUST campus in Thuwal, situated by the Red Sea. My research interests include computational statistics, fuzzy set theory, and time series data mining, among others. Outside of work, I am passionate about investing and travelling the world with my wife!

A bit on my background. I was born and grew up in Cedeira, a small and beautiful town located in northern Spain 🇪🇸, in a region called Galicia, which I encourage you to visit. I did my bachelor’s, master’s, and PhD degrees in Galicia. During my period as a PhD Candidate, I did research stays in Australia 🇦🇺, Germany 🇩🇪, Italy 🇮🇹, and the United Kingdom 🇬🇧. After receiving my PhD at age 28, I decided to leave Spain and move to Saudi Arabia.
